INFERNAL ESCAPE

Choose your hero from one of five factions and descend into the lair of Dod, the malevolent living cave corrupting your people and your homeland. But the way is not easy, for in The Pit lie monsters and mayhem and magical traps. Marshal your resources, work as a team, and save your home from Dod’s evil clutches in Underscore Games Infernal Escape.
